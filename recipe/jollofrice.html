Jollof Rice

Description

Jollof rice is a flavorful, spicy and delicious dish loved accross West african

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes,pepper,onions
  • oil
  • beef,fish,chicken
  • Rice
  • spices
  • salt/seasoning cubes

steps

  1. Blend the tomatoes and onions
  2. Put your pan on the fire, add oil and fry the tomatoes and onion paste
  3. Add your beef,fish or chicken stock
  4. add spice, salt and seasoning cube to taste
  5. Add your already parboiled rice, cook for 15min.
  6. Serve with your favorite drink/juice
