Fried Potatoes

Description

Learn to make the crispiest potatoes fries with this great recipe

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • oil
  • butter
  • salt

steps

  1. Wash and slice your potatoes into small slices
  2. Put your pan on the fire, add butter and oil to heat
  3. Add your potatoes
  4. Fliping to to the other side till golden brown or as desire
  5. serve with ketch up or any sauce of your choice
